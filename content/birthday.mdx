I'm that person who's not that interested in birthdays. Ever since I was a kid, I've seen my family struggle financially. That has kind of led me to compromise on a lot of things. Things that I really love, I've given up on because I didn't want to feel like a burden to my parents.

This May 2, 2026, I turned 26. I never thought I'd say this, but I was looking forward to this birthday. I remember spending my last birthday alone. This one was different. It's the people you spend time with—that's what matters.

To me, it was just one person who made it all different. I'm glad she's in my life.

I've got so much to do—for me, for her, for us.

I might have to spend many, many years just to break even with the amount of love and care she's given me.

If this is what every birthday is supposed to feel like, man, I love birthdays.
